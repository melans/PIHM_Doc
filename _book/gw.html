5.2 Horizontal flow

\[ \frac {dS_{gw}}{dt} = q_r + q_{gw}\]

where

  • \(S\) Ground water storage [\(m\)]
  • \(q_r\) Recharge to ground water [\(m/T\)]
  • \(q_{gw}\) Horizontal ground water flow at three directions [\(m^3/T\)] \[q_{gw} = \sum_{j=1}^3 Q_{j} / A\]
  • \(A\) Area of the elements \(i\), [\(m^2\)].
